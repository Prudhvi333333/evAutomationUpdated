## Question 10
Question: According to the Methodology sheet, how are supply chain tiers assigned (what approach is described)?

Answer:
The methodology for assigning supply chain tiers typically involves categorizing suppliers based on their directness to the original raw materials. Tier 1 suppliers are those that directly supply to the company. Tier 2 suppliers are those that supply to Tier 1 suppliers, and so on.
